Marvel Cinematic Universe: In Chronological Order

Captain America: The First Avenger

Starting the saga off in 1941...

Description: The year is 1941 and the world is in the throes of war. Steve Rogers wants to do his civil duty and join America's armed forces, but the military rejects him due to his small size. Finally, Steve is able to get his chance when he's accepted into an experimental program that ends up turning him into the one and only supersoldier we now know as Captain America. Joining forces with his best friend Bucky Barnes and Peggy Carter, Captain America leads the fight against the Nazi-backed HYDRA organization.

Captain Marvel

Description: Captain Marvel is an extraterrestrial Kree warrior who finds herself caught in the middle of an intergalactic battle between her people and the Skrulls. Living on Earth in 1995, Captain Marvel keeps having recurring memories of another life as U.S. Air Force pilot Carol Danvers. With help from Nick Fury, Captain Marvel works to uncover the secrets of her past while also harnessing her special superpowers to end the war with the "evil" Skrulls.

Iron Man

Description: Billionaire industrialist and genius inventor,Tony Stark, is conducting weapons tests overseas, but terrorists kidnap him to force him to build a devastating weapon. Instead though, he builds an armored suit and upends his captors. Returning to America, Stark refines the suit and uses it to combat crime and terrorism becoming the well known Iron Man.
